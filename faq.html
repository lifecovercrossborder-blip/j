FAQs: Frequently Asked Questions

If you need help, we have a list of frequently asked questions and answers. We strive to give you the best buying experience possible. Click a question below to view the answer.

1. How can I change something in my order? +

If you need to change something in your order, please contact us immediately. We usually process orders within 2-4 hours, and once we have processed your order, we will be unable to make any changes.

2. How can I pay for my order? +

We accept secure debit orders, Electronic Fund Transfers (EFT), and major credit card payments. All transactions are securely processed in South African Rands (ZAR). Monthly cover premiums are conveniently processed via automated monthly debit orders on your chosen calendar date.

3. Can I track my order? +

Yes. For physical purchases such as custom gravestones, memorial masonry, caskets, or floral arrangements, we send direct tracking notifications via SMS and email. Our logistics team will also coordinate directly with you to schedule professional masonry installation dates.

4. Can I return an item? +

Yes. Standard physical items can be returned within 14 days of delivery, provided they remain in their original, undamaged condition. Please note that customized memorial masonry, engraved tombstones, and tailored floral tributes are non-refundable once production or preparation has begun.

5. How long will my order take to be delivered? +

Polished coffins, caskets, and floral arrangements are typically dispatched within 24 to 48 hours for local ceremonies. Custom-carved gravestones and memorial masonry require professional stonemasonry craftsmanship and generally take between 2 to 4 weeks for complete preparation and on-site installation.

6. Do I need an account and what are the benefits? +

While you can complete purchases as a guest, creating an account allows you to securely manage your funeral cover policies, upload critical verification documents (such as passports or foreign IDs), review billing transactions, and access accelerated support during claim procedures.
