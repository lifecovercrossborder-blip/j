About Our Services

Providing compassionate planning, administrative transparency, and professional care to diaspora families across borders.

Memorial Masonry

Choose a memorial to remember your loved one. Our skilled craftsmen will help you design and prepare the perfect memorial, focusing on durable masonry options designed to endure.

After the Funeral

We will contact you after the funeral to make sure that you are satisfied with the service we provided. We can also help and advise you on the choice of memorial masonry, and discuss the scattering of the ashes.

Ask a Question

We answer some of the common questions people have about what to do and what happens at a funeral. Our support specialists remain available 24 hours to address critical inquiries.

Our History

on 02 May 1998

Our First Steps On The Market

Our history begins back in 1998, when our first specialized logistics offices opened to coordinate cross-border family support. The founders focused on offering consistent, reliable repatriation structures from day one.

on 07 March 2006

Expanded Border Channels

We established specialized transport links and administrative relationships across Southern African borders, ensuring families could complete final repatriation logistics quickly without delays.

on 30 January 2012

Underwritten Protection

To provide clients with absolute financial security, we formally aligned our policies with prominent, licensed underwriter insurers in South Africa, guaranteeing payments on all valid claims.
