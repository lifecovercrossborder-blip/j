Transparent Claims & Logistics Info

Clear policies underwritten for total financial and physical transport compliance [1].

Underwritten Waiting Periods

  • Immediate Cover: Guaranteed payout for claims resulting from accidental death after your first successful premium.
  • 6-Month Waiting Period: Applies to natural deaths (illness or age) standard under South African insurance regulations.
  • No Medical Checks: No physical examinations or medical histories are required to register.

Cross-Border Logistics Handled

  • Home Affairs Form DHA-1663: Official South African registration of death secured on your behalf.
  • Form BI-863 Clearance: Formal notification of death required for cross-border transport.
  • Embalming & Health Permits: Certified preparation and non-infectious disease clearances completed.
  • Consular Seal: Diplomatic transit permits arranged with the embassy of the destination country.

Claims Payout Guarantee

  • 24-Hour Payout: Cash benefits (such as Grocery/Airtime) processed within 24 hours of receiving validated claim documents [1].
  • Direct Support: Dedicated claims assistant assigned to manage border documentation.
  • No Hidden Costs: Clear flat-rate pricing with transparent, fixed monthly joining fees.
