Our Binding Guarantees

We understand the sensitivity of cross-border logistics. To provide complete peace of mind, every funeral policy is backed by these four operational guarantees .

1. Payout & Processing Guarantee

All valid claims for cash benefits (such as immediate Grocery or Airtime payouts) are processed and paid within 24 hours of receiving the fully certified documentary files from the beneficiary .

2. Administrative Logistics Guarantee

We guarantee full administrative handling of cross-border regulatory clearances, including the procurement of death certificates, Home Affairs removals, health permits, and consular clearance stamps .

3. Premium Level Guarantee

Your premium is based on your initial age band and chosen tier at registration. We guarantee your premium rates will not experience arbitrary spikes, helping you maintain consistent, reliable coverage.

4. Absolute Underwriting Stability

Every active policy is underwritten by a licensed, recognized corporate underwriter in compliance with the Financial Advisory and Intermediary Services (FAIS) Act, ensuring your coverage remains stable .

Important Compliance Note: All services are provided in strict accordance with SADC cross-border human remains transit regulations and South African Department of Health safety standards.
