Repatriation Logistics & SADC Standards

Cross-border repatriation involves intricate regulatory frameworks. Our specialized logistics network operates in full compliance with the South African Department of Health, Department of Home Affairs, and foreign consulates to ensure dignified transit across regional boundaries .

Southern Road Corridor (Direct Transit)

Zimbabwe

  • Border Post: Beitbridge
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 24 - 36 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Harare, Bulawayo, Gweru

Beitbridge represents one of the busiest regional border crossings. All transports require a certified embalming certificate, health permit clearance, and Zimbabwean consulate registration in Pretoria or Johannesburg prior to dispatch.

Lesotho

  • Border Post: Maseru Bridge / Ficksburg
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 12 - 18 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Maseru, Leribe, Mafeteng

Requires basic municipal clearance and certified South African health notifications. Border processes are generally streamlined, but custom transit clearances must be filed before arrival at the border gate.

Mozambique

  • Border Post: Lebombo / Ressano Garcia
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 18 - 30 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Maputo, Beira, Nampula

Mozambique transit requires complete translation of all South African administrative death forms (DHA-1663 & death certificate) into Portuguese, alongside official stamping by the Mozambican consulate.

Botswana

  • Border Post: Kopfontein / Ramatlabama
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 12 - 20 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Gaborone, Francistown

Botswana requires import permit permissions from local health boards. Rigorous health and sanitation compliance is enforced at both Kopfontein and Ramatlabama border checkpoints.

Namibia

  • Border Post: Nakop / Vioolsdrift
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 24 - 40 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Windhoek, Walvis Bay

Transits are handled via the Northern Cape routes. Namibia demands standard SADC health declarations, consular non-infectious disease certificates, and structured import manifest filings.

Eswatini

  • Border Post: Oshoek / Ngwenya
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 10 - 15 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Mbabane, Manzini

Eswatini transit benefits from proximity to South African logistics centers. Requires local chief notification confirmations and standard health clearance documents for smooth border clearing.

Inland Road Corridor (Extended SADC)

Malawi

  • Border Post: Dedza / Mwanza (Via Mozambique)
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 2 - 3 Days
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Lilongwe, Blantyre

As a landlocked country requiring passage through Mozambique or Zimbabwe, Malawi road transports require multiple transit permissions (cross-border customs manifestos) alongside standard Malawian health clearances.

Zambia

  • Border Post: Chirundu / Victoria Falls
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 36 - 48 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Lusaka, Ndola, Kitwe

Zambia road clearances are cleared directly through Zimbabwe. Local import permits must be secured from the Ministry of Health in Lusaka before our vehicles cross the Zambezi River.

Angola

  • Border Post: Santa Clara (Via Namibia)
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 3 - 4 Days (Or Air Freight)
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Luanda, Huambo, Lobito

Extremely long distance requires meticulous vehicle preparation or transition to direct air freight. All documents must be officially translated into Portuguese and legalised by the Angolan Embassy in Pretoria.

Tanzania

  • Border Post: Tunduma (Via Zambia)
  • Est. Road Transit Time: 4 - 5 Days (Or Air Freight)
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Dar es Salaam, Arusha, Mwanza

Crosses multiple international borders. Tanzania health authorities mandate sealed zinc-lined coffins, validated embalming treatments, and physical inspections at the Tunduma border entry post.

Central & East Africa Air Corridor

DR Congo

  • Transit Method: Air Freight (Standard) / Road
  • Est. Air Transit Time: 24 - 48 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Kinshasa, Lubumbashi, Goma

Air repatriation via OR Tambo International Airport is highly recommended for safety and preservation. Requires strict compliance with international IATA cargo rules, consulate seals, and import clearances.

Kenya

  • Transit Method: Air Freight (OR Tambo to JKIA)
  • Est. Air Transit Time: 24 - 48 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu

Coordination is completed with Kenya Airways or South African Airways cargo divisions. Standard documentation, embassy verification seals, and local receiver approvals are required at Nairobi JKIA cargo terminal.

Burundi

  • Transit Method: Air Freight (Bujumbura Airport)
  • Est. Air Transit Time: 36 - 72 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Bujumbura, Gitega

Air cargo space reservation must be verified 48 hours prior. Demands Ministry of Foreign Affairs clearance stamps, consular permission letters, and certified identification of the receiving funeral service.

Rwanda

  • Transit Method: Air Freight (Kigali International)
  • Est. Air Transit Time: 24 - 48 Hours
  • Primary Destination Hubs: Kigali, Gisenyi, Butare

Highly organized customs clearance. Rwanda mandates detailed digital health manifests, certified medical death causes, and official physical identification of the deceased by the Rwandan Embassy.

Required South African Documents

The following statutory clearances must be filed and approved before any human remains can leave South African jurisdiction :

  • Form DHA-1663: Official Registration of Death issued by a certified medical practitioner .
  • BI-863 Notification of Death: Filed with Home Affairs to obtain the official Death Certificate .
  • Form GW 11/14 (Removal Permit): Authorized and signed by a municipal health officer .
  • Non-Infectious Disease Declaration: Issued by the attending doctor or local pathologist .
  • Embalming Certificate: Legally required for all human remains crossing borders .

Technical Transit Specifications

We maintain absolute adherence to health standards to guarantee clean border inspections and dignified carriage:

  • Hermetic Zinc Lining: Legally mandated for air and international road transport. The inner zinc container is hermetically soldered and sealed within the wooden casket.
  • Refrigerated Vehicles: Fleet utilizes customized, long-distance road transport fitted with active temperature control systems.
  • Consular Seals: Embassies place wax security seals on the transport casket following official documentation verification.
  • Border Clearing Agents: Pre-retained clearing officials coordinate direct gate transits at Beitbridge, Lebombo, and Maseru.
